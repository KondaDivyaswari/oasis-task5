Yoga

Get ready to build strength in this Pilates Yoga Workout! The class starts in tabletop to flow through cat/cow and spinal waves into cobra pose. The warm up continues with kneeling side body stretches and mini camel heart-openers. Rest in child's pose with a deep shoulder stretch and then hold downward facing dog. Practice three-legged dog, pyramid, high lunge, warrior II, triangle, reverse triangle and low lunge twist. Open the chest with cobra, upward facing dog and melting heart pose. The class ends with a seated neck stretch and a few minutes of quiet, mindful reflection.
